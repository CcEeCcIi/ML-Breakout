## AI training

  *Agent*: paddle <br />
  *Action Inputs*: left, right <br />
  
  *Observations*: paddle position, ball position, add in camera sensor <br />
  *Reward*: catch a ball, hit a brick <br />
  
  *Training time*: 8M steps <br />
